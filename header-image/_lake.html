Laguna Brava Lake

Laguna Brava is a lake of incomparable beauty located in western Guatemala

Laguna Brava is a karstic lake, which means it was formed by the dissolution of soluble rocks such as limestone. The lake's beauty lies in the color variety of its waters, which depends on the sun's position.

Laguna Brava is also known as Yolnabaj by the region's inhabitants, which translates as "rough lake." Despite its name, the waters of Laguna Brava Laguna are so calm that they instantly awaken a deep feeling of peace.
