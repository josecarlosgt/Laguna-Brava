Services

The communities around Laguna Brava offer basic services to its visitors

Laguna Brava is located in a remote and highly underdeveloped region. Reaching and exploring the lake takes a mix of extreme adventure and intimate contact with nature.

Transport

To get to Laguna Brava on Public Transportation, you’ll need to take several buses or microbuses. At each place where you need to change buses, you can ask for the “terminal de buses” and say the name of your next destination, for example: “¿Bus a Nentón?” People are generally helpful.

Accommodation

From mid-March until June, the water levels at Laguna Brava go down, and beaches form on the lakeshore, a gorgeous place to camp.

Food

Make sure you bring enough water, a water filter, and all the food you need.
